Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Frigate Notify Bridge is an open-source Home Assistant integration that delivers push notifications from a self-hosted Frigate NVR instance to the Frigate Mobile app. It runs entirely on your own hardware.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The integration runs locally on your Home Assistant instance. No data is sent to us or any third party operated by us.

Google Account Access

During the optional Easy Setup flow, the integration requests temporary access to your Google account to:

  • List your Firebase projects
  • Enable the Firebase Cloud Messaging API on your selected project
  • Create a service account key for sending push notifications

This access is used only during initial setup. The OAuth access token is revoked immediately after setup completes. The generated service account key is stored locally on your Home Assistant instance and is never transmitted to us.

Push Notifications

Notifications are sent directly from your Home Assistant instance to Google's Firebase Cloud Messaging service (or ntfy/Pushover, depending on your configuration). We do not operate any relay servers and have no access to your notification content.

Third-Party Services

Depending on your chosen push provider, notifications pass through:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — operated by Google (Google Privacy Policy)
  • ntfy — self-hosted or via ntfy.sh (ntfy Privacy Policy)
  • Pushover — operated by Superblock (Pushover Privacy Policy)

Open Source

This integration is fully open source. You can review the complete source code at github.com/LowkeyNEXT/frigate-notify-bridge.
